## How to read the image notes

Every image is tagged so you know what's safe to publish:

- **✅ MINE** — your own photos or work. Safe to publish.
- **🔒 PRIVACY** — your own photos, but they show children's faces. Safe to *own*, but blur/crop faces or use back-of-head / hands-only shots before publishing.
- **⚠️ PERMISSION** — someone else's photo, artwork, or a film/video screenshot. Fine as private reference; **needs the creator's permission (or a paid license) before it goes on a public page.** Otherwise swap for a cleared/stock image.

You asked me to plan with *all* images for now, so I've placed the strongest one for each slot and flagged what to clear before launch.
